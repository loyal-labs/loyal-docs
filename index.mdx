## Introduction

You are stepping into a network built to end surveillance-era AI. Centralized providers harvest your prompts, personal data, and decisions to feed their models. Loyal replaces that bargain with hardware-attested agents, encrypted micropayments, and a peer-to-peer marketplace where you stay in control of what you share.
