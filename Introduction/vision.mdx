## Why You Need Loyal

You do not owe your private thoughts to advertising platforms and government agencies. Loyal exists so you can reliably access intelligence without surrendering identity, context, or intent. The manifesto insists that privacy is earned through math, not policy, and that you have a responsibility to ship the code that enforces it.
